Great Clutchness and Leadership

Dame wasn't just about stats. He was also about the moments. Dame time, walk-offs, and leading an entire franchise with zero complaints. Who else is gonna pull up from logo waving goodbye to entire teams in the playoffs like it's an off season match? He man had ice in his veins. You want proof? Let's talk about his 61-point game in the Bubble, or his dagger against Paul George and the Thunder. That shot wasn't during Bubble Dame but that deep 3 is the start of his prime, anyways He didn't even blink. That's being a leader. That's being a killer. That's the worst player of all time, that's the MVP, PRIME DAMIAN LILLARD. And let's not overlook his leadership. While other stars needed superteams to get anywhere, Lillard carried a flawed Blazers squad to multiple deep playoff runs. He held teammates accountable, motivated an entire fanbase, and never backed down from a challenge. That kind of mentality? It's rare.

Legacy.

We should all look back on his name, and respect that man, his name is Lillard. His loyalty, elite offensive ability, playoff heroics, and leadership should put him on a higher pedestal than many of the other point guards throughout. The man defined what it means to be a killer on the court. He didn’t just play basketball—he lowkey made history. So next time someone dares to disrespect Lillard's name, remind them: Bubble Lillard. 2019-2020 Lillard. The Man. The Myth. The Legend.
